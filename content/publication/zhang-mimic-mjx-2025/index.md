abstract: We present MIMIC-MJX, a framework for neuromechanical emulation of animal
  behavior using physics-based simulation. MIMIC-MJX combines musculoskeletal models
  with neural control policies trained via deep imitation learning to reproduce naturalistic
  animal locomotion and movement. The framework bridges neuroscience and biomechanics
  by enabling closed-loop simulations that account for both neural and mechanical
  constraints on behavior.
