abstract: Using connectome-constrained simulations of the Drosophila nervous system,
  we identify a central pattern generator (CPG) circuit underlying fly walking. By
  simulating neural dynamics within the connectome and coupling them to a biomechanical
  body model, we characterize how specific circuit motifs generate the rhythmic motor
  patterns required for locomotion.
